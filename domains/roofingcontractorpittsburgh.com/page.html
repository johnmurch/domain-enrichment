Signs You Need Roof Repair in Pittsburgh

The average asphalt shingle roof tends to last for roughly 20 to 30 years, depending on the climate and preventive maintenance performed on the residence. However, there are plenty of other factors that impact the lifespan of your roof, including the quality of the shingles, the installation method itself, whether the roof is properly ventilated, it’s slope & orientation, and more!

Damage to your roof can reveal itself in a variety of ways, from missing or broken shingles, to loose granules, to leaks that appear on the inside of your home. Considering how important your roof is to the health of your home, functioning as its first line of defense against inclement weather, it is important to ensure that the system is kept in good repair! If you need to fix your home’s roof in Pittsburgh, our team of roofers is happy to be able to offer our roof repair & replacement services to the community. A small issue can quickly turn into major repairs, which is why it’s important to maintain your roof with proper preventive care, and reach out to a professional like our team at Doing It Right anytime you suspect your roof is damaged.

Our team will take a look at your roof and advise you on whether you can repair the roof & get a few more years out of it, or if you’re better off replacing the system for your home’s health. We’ll work with you to find the perfect roofing material for your home!
